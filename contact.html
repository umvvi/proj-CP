My joints fell apart what glue should i use? or is ductape more effective?

In the case that your joints are falling apart effectively dislocating there are a few measures you can take to ensure not only your safety but your comfort as well.

  • glue. the most effective glue is super glue. this is by far most effective and comfortable.
  • ductape is a very versatile but less comforting alternative to glue it is very effective but could cause some irritation.
  • there are also alternatives such as a rope, nails, welding, a cast or cement although we do not advice using them.

  • but why is this happening?
  • Arthritis occurs due to a combination of factors, including:
  • Genetics: Certain genetic traits can make a person more susceptible to arthritis.
  • Injury: Joint damage from a previous injury or overuse can contribute to the development of arthritis.
  • Abnormal Metabolism: Issues with the metabolism can lead to the development of gout and pseudogout.
  • Infections: Some infections can lead to the development of arthritis.
  • Immune System Dysfunction: The body's immune system can mistakenly attack the synovium (the lining of the membranes that surround the joints), leading to rheumatoid arthritis.
  • Age: The risk of developing many types of arthritis increases with age.
  • Lifestyle Factors: Factors such as excess weight, joint injuries, and occupations that involve repetitive motions can contribute to arthritis
